## Section: How we're different

**End-to-end, no handoffs.** Design, backend, growth — under one delivery contract. No agency chain, no scope walls.

**Compliance-first architecture.** Engineered to align with ISO 9001, ISO 27001, GDPR, and Bangladesh PDPA 2026. Sovereign cloud data residency for regulated tenants.

**ScaleGate telemetry.** Every client gets a portal with real-time developer commits, sprint capacity, and page-performance tracking. No opaque invoices, no black-box progress.

**Dual-entity financial pipeline.** Seamless cross-border contracting and billing. Continuous access to global infrastructure. No remittance friction.
