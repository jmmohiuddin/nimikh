# Guest Post & Podcast Pitch Templates

Send one guest-post pitch per week and one podcast pitch per week. Track responses in a Google Sheet.

---

## Guest post pitch template — Tier A engineering publications

**Targets:** Smashing Magazine, LogRocket, InfoQ, The New Stack, DZone, Dev.to (editorial), freeCodeCamp News.

**Subject line:** "Pitch: [Specific topic + audience benefit] — for [publication]"

**Example subject:** "Pitch: PostgreSQL Row-Level Security for Multi-Tenant SaaS — for Smashing Magazine"

**Body:**

> Hi [editor first name],
>
> I've been reading [publication] for [X] years and I noticed you haven't covered [specific gap] recently — the last piece I could find is [linked article + year].
>
> I'd like to pitch: **[working title]**.
>
> The angle: [one-sentence thesis].
>
> The audience benefit: [specific reader outcome — what they'll be able to do after reading].
>
> Rough structure (2,500 words):
>
> 1. [Section 1]
> 2. [Section 2]
> 3. [Section 3]
> 4. [Section 4]
> 5. [Section 5]
>
> Original elements:
> - [Original data, benchmark, or code sample I'll include].
> - [Diagrams I'll produce].
> - [Runnable GitHub repo I'll ship alongside].
>
> Why me: I'm the founder of Nimikh (nimikh.com), where we engineer [relevant systems]. I've shipped this pattern in [count] production systems including [named use case if allowed].
>
> Portfolio: [Link to two of my strongest published pieces]. If those aren't the right style, happy to send a draft on spec.
>
> Reply-friendly time zone: BST (UTC+6). I'll turn a draft in [7] days from your green light.
>
> Thanks,
> Mohiuddin
> Founder & CEO, Nimikh — nimikh.com
> https://www.linkedin.com/in/jm-mohiuddin/
